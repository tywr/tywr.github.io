I am 29, and I have been playing bass guitar for almost a decade by now, almost on a daily basis. I like to play, but I also like to buy gear. Some describe this phenomenon as GAS – the Gear Acquisition Syndrome. When you've been playing your instrument for some time, you like to start experimenting with the sound of it, and for guitarists, it always comes down to amplification and effect pedals. In my case, I like simplicity, but I enjoy trying out new effects, and have found my special gem : an overdrive pedal called Darkglass Vintage Microtubes.
